How to Play...
  1. Press 'play' to start the game
  2. Select your difficulty level...

    Rick
    (3x3)

    Morty
    (4x4)

  3. Take a good look at the image, remember what it looks like.
  4. Click 'shuffle' to scramble the image.
  5. Tap to move the tiles and unscramble the puzzle.
  6. Complete the puzzle to unlock the bonus.
  7. Complete the bonus puzzle to 'Get Schwifty!'.
In-game
  • Click 'shuffle' at any time to reset the puzzle.
  • Click on the portal at any time to return to the menu screen.
  • If you can't remember what to do, click on the meeseeks box for a helpful reminder.
  • Click on the musical notes to adjust, or turn on/off the in-game audio.
