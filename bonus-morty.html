How to Play...
  1. Take a good look at the image, remember what it looks like.
  2. Click 'shuffle' to scramble the image.
  3. Move the tiles to unscramble the picture and complete the puzzle.
  4. Complete all the puzzles to unlock the bonus puzzle.
  5. Complete the bonus puzzle to win the game.
In-game
  • Click 'shuffle' at any time to reset the puzzle.
  • Click on the portal to return to the menu screen.
  • If you can't remember what to do, click on the meeseeks box for a helpful reminder.
  • Click on the musical notes to adjust, or turn on/off the in-game audio.
